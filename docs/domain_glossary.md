# Domain Glossary

## Orders
A commitment by the customer to purchase specific products or services. An order can be paid in full, reserved, or pending confirmation.

## Checkout
The process of calculating the total cost, confirming product availability, and finalizing payment.

## Inventory
The catalog of available solar panels, batteries, inverters, accessories, and installation services in the store.

## Scenario
A pre-configured customer situation that describes the customer's goal, product knowledge level, payment status, and constraints.

## Transcript
The textual record of both the sales associate and the simulated customer, including auto-transcribed speech.

## Simulation Session
One complete conversation from start to evaluation. A session includes scenario data, transcript, and performance scoring.

## Installation Package
A bundled offering that includes solar panels, inverter, battery, mounting, wiring, and optional installation service.

## Technical Inquiry
A scenario where the customer asks compatibility, sizing, or specification questions about solar components.

## Ideal Resolution
The target outcome for a scenario (e.g., the customer agrees to a package, schedules installation, or receives a clear technical answer).

## Evaluation Score
A numeric 1-5 rating across multiple criteria (needs discovery, product matching, etc.) with an overall average score.
